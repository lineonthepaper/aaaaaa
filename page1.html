ElderGuard

Purpose

  1. for: Elderly persons who live alone
  2. who: Ensure physical safety without the need of a caregiver
  3. the device: Detects when and where elderly people fall at home using a sound sensor
  4. unlike: HPB fitness tracker
  5. our device:
    1. logs down the location of the fall
    2. alerts passerby for help
    3. simple to set up and use
    4. small and portable

Our device is unintrusive and functions 24/7

Features And Benefits

  1. Sound sensor — Able to immediately recognize when elderly is in distress, ensures a guaranteed response
  2. Alarm & LED — Notify nearby passerby to render assistance, easy to read and understand distress signal
  3. Datalogger — Records past incidents for future safety analysis
